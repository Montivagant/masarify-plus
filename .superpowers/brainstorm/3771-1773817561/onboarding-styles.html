A. Gradient Immersive

Full-bleed gradient backgrounds with bold type. Decorative geometric shapes. High-energy, startup feel. Think Revolut / Monzo onboarding.

Pros

  • Eye-catching first impression
  • Strong brand color presence
  • Trendy gradient-mesh aesthetic

Cons

  • Needs custom illustrations per page
  • Heavier than minimal approach

B. Glass Card (Extends Masarify DS)

Builds on existing glassmorphism design system. Frosted cards over soft gradient mesh. Consistent with the app's GlassCard/GlassTier system.

Pros

  • Consistent with existing design system
  • Reuses GlassCard components
  • Elegant, premium feel

Cons

  • Less "wow" factor than gradient
  • May feel too subtle for first impression

C. Bold Minimal (Apple-style)

Near-white background, large Lottie illustrations as the star. Bold typography with short punchy copy. Apple/Notion onboarding vibes.

Pros

  • Maximum focus on Lottie animations
  • Clean, modern, high-end feel
  • Easiest to implement

Cons

  • May feel too plain without animations
  • Dark theme version needs more thought
  • Breaks from app's glass identity
